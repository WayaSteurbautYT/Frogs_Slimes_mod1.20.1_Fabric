# Potion Brewing - Frog & Slime Gamemode

## Frog Potion Stand

The Frog Potion Stand is a custom brewing station that allows you to brew special mod potions using vanilla ingredients.

## Available Potions

### Frog Agility Potion
Brewed using: Slime Ball + Speed Potion
- **Effect:** Speed II + Jump Boost II for 3 minutes
- **Color:** Green

### Slime Resistance Potion
Brewed using: Slime Ball + Resistance Potion
- **Effect:** Resistance II + Slime immunity for 3 minutes
- **Color:** Lime Green

### Frog Tongue Potion
Brewed using: Spider Eye + Potion of Leaping
- **Effect:** Can grab enemies from distance (tongue grab ability) for 2 minutes
- **Color:** Purple

### Evolution Elixir
Brewed using: Evolution Stone + Golden Apple + Potion of Regeneration
- **Effect:** Instant evolution of your helper to next stage + Regeneration III
- **Color:** Golden

### Ender Slime Potion
Brewed using: Ender Pearl + Slime Ball + Potion of Invisibility
- **Effect:** Teleportation + Invisibility for 2 minutes
- **Color:** Dark Purple

### Poison Slime Potion
Brewed using: Spider Eye + Slime Ball + Potion of Poison
- **Effect:** Poison II + Slowness II on enemies for 45 seconds
- **Color:** Dark Green

## Brewing Process

1. Place Frog Potion Stand
2. Add glass bottles (up to 3)
3. Add brewing ingredients in the top slot
4. Wait for brewing to complete
5. Collect potions

## Notes

- All potions are brewed using the Frog Potion Stand (crafted with Slime Balls + Brewing Stand)
- Some potions require mod-specific items (Evolution Stone, Slime Ball)
- Potions have custom effects not available in vanilla brewing
- Brewing time is slightly faster than vanilla brewing stand
