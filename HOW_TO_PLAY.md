# Frog & Slime Gamemode - How to Play

## Overview
Frog & Slime Gamemode is a Minecraft mod where you tame frog and slime helpers that fight for you and evolve as they kill mobs. The mod has an unexpected twist at the end when you defeat the Ender Dragon.

## Getting Started

### Activating the Gamemode
1. Join a server with the mod installed
2. Use the command `/frogslime enable` to activate the gamemode
3. You'll receive a message confirming activation

### Taming Helpers
Once the gamemode is active, you can tame helpers:

**Frog Helper:**
- Find a frog in the world
- Right-click with an empty hand to tame
- Frog helpers attack hostile mobs and evolve by killing them

**Slime Helper:**
- Find a slime in the world
- Right-click with an empty hand to tame
- Slime helpers are tankier and have a special final evolution

## Evolution System

### Frog Helper Evolution
- **Stage 0 → Stage 1:** Kill 10 mobs
- **Stage 1 → Stage 2:** Kill 25 mobs
- **Stage 2 → Stage 3:** Kill 50 mobs

Each evolution increases:
- Health (+10 per stage)
- Attack Damage (+2 per stage)
- New visual effects at higher stages

### Slime Helper Evolution
- **Stage 0 → Stage 1:** Kill 15 mobs
- **Stage 1 → Stage 2:** Kill 35 mobs
- **Stage 2 → Stage 3:** Kill 60 mobs

Each evolution increases:
- Health (+15 per stage)
- Attack Damage (+3 per stage)
- Knockback resistance

### Final Evolution (Slime Only)
After defeating the Ender Dragon, use the **Final Evolution Crystal** on your slime helper to unlock its ultimate form with massive stats:
- 200 Health
- 30 Attack Damage
- 100% Knockback Resistance

## Items & Crafting

### Evolution Items
- **Evolution Stone:** Instantly evolve your helper (diamond + slime ball)
- **Final Evolution Crystal:** Unlocks ultimate slime form (obtained after dragon kill)

### Food Items
- **Slime Food:** Feed to slime helper (slime ball + rabbit)
- **Frog Food:** Feed to frog helper (spider eye + cooked chicken)

### YouTuber Swords (Special Weapons)
- **Dream Sword:** Netherite sword with gold accents
- **Technoblade Sword:** Netherite sword with crown and bone decorations
- **Grian Sword:** Diamond sword with prismarine crystals
- **Mumbo Jumbo Sword:** Diamond sword with redstone circuits

### Funny Armor
- **Mustard Helmet:** Yellow helmet
- **Orphan Shield:** Gold and iron shield
- **Prankster Chestplate:** Purple jester chestplate

### Utility
- **Task Book:** View quests and objectives

## Viewing Stats
Right-click your helper while sneaking with an empty hand to view:
- Current evolution stage
- Total mobs killed
- Attack damage

## Controls
- **Right-click (empty hand):** Tame helper
- **Right-click (sneaking, empty hand):** View stats
- **Right-click with item:** Use item on helper (evolution stone, food, etc.)

## Tips
- Tame multiple helpers for faster progression
- Use evolution stones to skip kill requirements
- Feed your helpers food to keep them strong
- The slime helper is tankier but evolves slower
- The frog helper evolves faster but is less durable

## Recipes
All recipes unlock automatically when you obtain the required materials in your inventory. Check the recipe book (E key) for crafting patterns.

## Server Requirements
- Minecraft 1.20.1
- Fabric Loader
- Required Fabric API dependencies

## Troubleshooting
- If helpers appear black, ensure resource pack is loaded
- If recipes don't show, pick up the required materials
- If taming doesn't work, ensure gamemode is active with `/frogslime enable`
